About Me

I am a full time Staff at the Indiana Geological Survey at Indiana University and consultant for the Research Technologies Division of UITS. I also instruct the IT Training Workshops at IU and IUPUI for GIS (Geographic Information Systems) software. I specialize in developing interactive GIS websites and applications for web and mobile clients.

I just completed a Master of Information Science from the School of Informatics and Computing as a part time graduate student. Outside of work and school, My wife April and I have two sons, Elija and Miles, and operate a small cattle and poultry farm in Morgan County, Indiana.
