Welcome

This site is created as an online presence to showcase some of my previous work and current interests.





Having spent many years performing GIS and cartographic work, I have had a interest in graphically representing and visualizing information for some time. With a primary interest in Geographic Information Science, I am also interested in Information Visualization. With many of the concepts of the Information Visualization field born from Geography, I am intrigued by what the two fields have in common and what each can learn from the other.


Please take a few moments to browse the site and learn about me, my educational experience at Indiana University, and my portfolio of professional projects that I am currently working on as a staff member of The Pervasive Technology Institute at Indiana University and a member of the Research Analytics team of Research Technologies at UITS.
